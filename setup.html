SirusPTB OTA Firmware Update Guide

⚠️ Important Notice
When updating firmware on the SirusPTB you need to ensure the device doesn't lose power during the update so make sure to fully charge the device before starting the update. The device will reboot automatically after a successful update.

1Download Firmware

First, download the latest firmware file to your computer or mobile device.

⬇️ Download SirusPTB.bin

Once download has completed go on to step 2

2Connect to Your SirusPTB

Connect your computer or mobile device to the same network as your SirusPTB.

  • Check your computer or phone Wifi list
  • Look for the Network named SirusPTB-OTA
  • Join the network. Don't worry if it says no internet.

3Access the OTA Web Interface

Click this link OTA begin page or type the following into the address bar of your web browser

192.168.4.1
⚡ Critical: Charge to 100% before starting
Keep the SirusPTB as close as possible (within 1 meter) to the device you are using to update the firmware.
