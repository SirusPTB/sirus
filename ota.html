SirusPTB Firmware Update

To update your SirusPTB firmware, follow these steps:

  1. Click the button below to download the latest firmware file.
  2. Once downloaded, connect your Wi-Fi to SirusPTB-OTA.
  3. Click this link: http://esp32.local.
  4. Upload the firmware file and follow the on-screen instructions.

Download Firmware
⚠️ Important:
  • Do not disconnect your device during the update.
  • Ensure your device remains powered on throughout the process.
  • Make sure the battery is fully charged before starting.
Interrupting the update may cause firmware corruption or device malfunction.
